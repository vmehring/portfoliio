Triangle Treatment Tango

T3 is a design for stormwater treatment wetlands at Magnuson Park. This was a complex project that required attention to numerous criteria, but the most critical aspect was the grading and drainage at the site and its immediate surroundings. It was necessary to design the grading to allow water to flow through a series of detention ponds, each one in turn filtering out more sediment and pollutants before draining into Lake Washington.
